Friendly Chemist

Friendly Chemist (real name Jean Brazeau) is a producer & DJ currently based in Vancouver, Canada.

 

While his live performances consist mostly of antiquated analog hardware, his productions are lush, distinctively influenced by both late 20th century Techno and contemporary Pop music.

Friendly Chemist is currently available for both Live PA performances as well as DJ sets
